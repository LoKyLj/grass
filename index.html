Why artificial turf?

Elastic and flexible

The elastic and flexible structure of the grass instantly regains its shape after pressure, providing a durable and resilient cover

UV protection

A high degree of protection against UV rays, which guarantees durability and color fastness even under intensive exposure to sunlight

Kids friendly

A safe and well-maintained place to play and relax. The soft surface reduces the risk of injury and the grass structure creates a pleasant feeling underfoot

Weather resistant

Resistant from high temperatures to heavy rains. Grass retains its shape and color, providing consistent beauty in any season

Fire resistant

High resistance to fire, which provides an extra layer of protection in case of unexpected situations

Maintained appearance

Specially developed technology maintains pile height and shape while maintaining a natural and attractive look

Minimal maintenance

Almost zero maintenance required. Forget about watering and lawn mowing

Pet friendly

Created with consideration of your pet’s care, offering a secure and cozy surface
